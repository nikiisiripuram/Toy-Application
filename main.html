STUFFED TOYS

A stuffed toy, also known as a plush toy, is a soft, cuddly toy made from fabric and filled with a soft material like cotton, synthetic fibers, or other similar materials. These toys are designed to resemble animals, fictional characters, or other objects, and they often serve as comforting companions for children and even adults.
